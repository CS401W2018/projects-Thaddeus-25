My Hero - Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Interest Hero

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was a composer of the early Classical period. I listened to his pieces since I was young and I still love to listen to it. My parents were the ones who intoruced me to the works of Mozart. The pieces he composed has inspired me to play the piano.

Birth
He was born in 27 January 1756, Salzburg.
Total of pieces he composed
He composed more than 800 pieces many which are symphonic, concertante, chamber, operatic, and choral repertoire.
My favorite pieces
Eine kleine Nachtmusik, Symphony No.40 in G minor K.550, The Marriage of Figaro.
