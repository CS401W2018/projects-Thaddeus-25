My Favorite Monster

Xeno'jiiva

Xeno'jiiva is a monster that was first introduced in this installment. It is classified as an elder dragon and is the final boss for the game's story mode. It's appearance is unusual as it has six orange sensory organs on its face. Its skin is blue and kind of trnaslucent, giving of a extraterrestrial look. When it is enraged its body glows red and orange. Source: monsterhunterworld.wiki
