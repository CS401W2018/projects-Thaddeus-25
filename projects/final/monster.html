My Favorite Monster

Xeno'jiiva

Xeno'jiiva is a monster that was first introduced in this installment. It is classified as an elder dragon and is the final boss for the game's story mode. It's appearance is unusual as it has six orange sensory organs on its face. Its skin is blue and kind of translucent, giving of a extraterrestrial look. When it is enraged its body glows red and orange. When it reaches adulthood its skin will turn red and Xeno'jiiva will turn into a Safi'jiiva. Source: monsterhunterworld.wiki

Xeno'jiiva is a powerful monster that uses bioenergy to power itself. It absorbs bioenergy from the energy around it and elder dragon that it lures. After absorbing enough energy, it can unleash an explosive burst of flames that could instantly kill hunters who come into contact with it.
