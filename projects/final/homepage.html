What is Monster Hunter World

Monster Hunter World is a video game developed and published by Capcom, a japanese video game company. It is the fifth installment in the monster hunter series. released in 2018, being one of the best open world multiplayer game.

Players will take on a role as a hunter tasked to track and hunt down giant monsters. The game allows players to use various weapons and gears to defeat the monster. Not only do players hunt monsters, they can also enjoy the beautiful nature of the maps.

Story

Unlike previous installments this game has a story provided for players to follow. It begins with a group of hunters who were commisioned to explore the New World. This place is where elder dragons would migrate to once every decade. To understand this phenomenon, the guild sent hunters to research the place. They were in pursuit of a colossal elder dragon known as Zorah Magdoras. This will lead the hunters to many discovery and mysteries that is yet to be found.
