How to play

This game is an action role-playing game where players control their hunter and create a sidekick to play along with. Players are allowed to choose various weapons depending on their gameplay style. Players can also take advantage of the environments and wildlife to assist them in their hunts. (Source: monsterhunter.fandom)

First players can choose the quest they want to embark on, whether it be gathering certain items, slaying or capturing monster(s), or just exploring areas at their own pace. Before departing players are required to select the weapons they want to use. During a quest players are given a time limit to complete the quest
