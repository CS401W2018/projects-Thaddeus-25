About the family

Mr Turner

Mr Turner is Timmy Turner's father. Most commonly known as "Timmy's Dad". Mr. Turner is commonly shown as a man wearing office shirts and blue pants with a necktie, a black belt and black shoes.

Timmy Turner

Timmy Turner is the main character of the show. He is often seen alongside his godparents Cosmos and Wanda, which is able to grant him wishes. Timmy wears a pink hat which matches his shirt, and has blue pants and shoes.

Mrs Turner

Mrs Turner is the wife of Mr Turner. She is usually refered as "Timmy's Mom" or "Timmy's Mother". She is depicted as a dimwitted and oblivious person in the show.
